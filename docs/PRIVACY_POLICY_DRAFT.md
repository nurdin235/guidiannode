# GuardianNode Privacy Policy Draft

This draft is a starting point for legal review before Play Store submission.

GuardianNode helps Bamenda residents send emergency alerts, share emergency location context, and notify trusted contacts or nearby responders.

## Information We Collect

- Account details, including name, phone number, and neighborhood.
- Emergency contact details, including contact name, relationship, and phone number.
- Location data when you enable location permissions or trigger an emergency alert.
- Emergency alert details, including alert type, status, timestamps, and approximate incident location.
- Device/network metadata needed to operate authentication, security, and API services.

## How We Use Information

- To verify account access through an inbound WhatsApp message.
- To create and manage emergency alerts.
- To route nearby help and display live map context.
- To notify emergency contacts or nearby community responders where supported.
- To maintain security, prevent misuse, and improve service reliability.

## Location Data

GuardianNode uses location to support emergency routing, nearby alert discovery, responder guidance, and live SOS updates. Location is most important during an active emergency. Users can disable location permissions at the device level.

## Sharing

Emergency-related data may be shared with trusted contacts, nearby responders, and backend services required to operate the app. GuardianNode does not sell personal data.

## Security

GuardianNode uses authenticated API requests and server-issued sessions. WhatsApp verification tokens are short-lived, stored as hashes, and accepted only from the phone number being verified.
